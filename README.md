This was an idea I had because I have little discipline and I wanted to finally start doing my flexibility routine.
Let's say you want to do 15 minutes of flashcards daily at 8pm. 
You set a scheduler so that you're forced to do 15 minutes of Anki, otherwise the rest of your phone is locked until you do it (except for vital/emergency apps etc)
Once you complete it, your phone is released from the overlay.
